About CU FIRST

CU FIRST is Carleton’s volunteer-led hub for FIRST Robotics alumni and supporters. We mentor local teams, host on-campus outreach like the Fall Showcase and the FRC Kickoff, and partner with Carleton’s Faculty of Engineering and Design to welcome future engineers to campus.

Mentorship

Support FRC/FTC/FLL teams across the National Capital Region with technical guidance, workshops, and off‑season projects.

On‑Campus Events

Bring robotics to Carleton—test fields, scrimmages, and the official FRC Kickoff—so high‑school students experience university labs first‑hand.

Community & Careers

Link students with alumni and industry sponsors through talks, tours, and recruiting activities.
